About Our Recommend System

Welcome to our Emotion-aware Movie Recommendation System.

If you are using our system for the first time, there are some rules you need to understand. Our system is based on the user's current facial emotion to recommend movies for users, so please make sure that the camera is turned on and can get all your facial information.

In our system, you must complete a questionnaire before recommending movies to users. This questionnaire determines the emotional orientation of recommending movies for you. When you complete this questionnaire, you will automatically jump to the recommendation page. There will be four movies recommended based on your current emotions. You can use the button to go to the details page for more information. You can also search for specific movies through search page.
